About Me


Hello, my name is Jordan Burrell and I am an aspiring web developer. My experience includes 9 years as a funeral home assistant and 2 years as chief operating officer of Terry Funeral Home Inc. 2 years as a realtor at Berkshire Hathaway Fox Roach Realtors. 1 year of leasing consulting. I am now a certified personal trainer through ISSA, a corrective exercise specialist and have also received a certificate from Stanford University. I am currently the Fitness Director at the Willow Grove YMCA. I am from Glenside, PA, and received my BA in Economics from Morehouse College. My hobbies included working out and all things active.
